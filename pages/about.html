About

VINTAGESTAR is a company well established by its owner Mrs. Bridget Jones,born in South Africa and raised in Jackson Mississippi,USA, in 1992. Mrs.Jones started this now popular vintage shop in the year 2015 after the tragic passing of her grandfather. She was inspired by a vinyl shop he had years back from the 1970s and aspired to lead a legacy set by her grandfather and inspire youth with vintage products.Vintagstar has a physicals stores in Jackson Mississippi as well as in Cape Town,South Africa, but wants to connect with people of the world and have as many people access the quality products they produce and sell. For the first time people will be able to sell,and share their most tresured items. Thrifting has taken a different form! We hope you can be a regular customer!
